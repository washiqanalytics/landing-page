Suds Deluxe Car Wash

Scaling Across Central TX and Houston with Intelligence at Scale

Suds Deluxe operates 15 tunnels across two major Texas markets and needed a platform that could keep pace with their growth. With locations spread across Central Texas and Houston, leadership needed a unified view of performance and the tools to drive execution at scale. WashIQ integrated directly with their Sonny's POS and unified the entire portfolio under one platform — real-time visibility across every site, every shift, every metric. AI-powered analysis surfaced over $500K in recoverable revenue through subscription conversion improvements and premium package optimization. Hourly and daily leaderboards supercharged team performance — ranking employees by subscriptions sold and premium attach rates pushed top two package mix up 3% across the portfolio, translating directly to higher revenue per car.

Bubble Bay Car Wash

Commanding Operations with Real-Time Visibility

Bubble Bay Car Wash had a solid operation but lacked real-time visibility into hourly conversion rates, staffing efficiency, and throughput — meaning opportunities were slipping through the cracks every shift. WashIQ gave ownership a command center. Hourly sales and conversion alerts transformed how the team managed membership attach rates, delivering a 4% lift in subscription conversions. Time tracking alerts eliminated payroll leakage from unauthorized early clock-ins and late clock-outs, while productivity monitoring right-sized every shift to match actual demand — cutting payroll costs 3%. Automated operational alerts caught throughput drops in real time, turning end-of-day surprises into same-hour interventions.
